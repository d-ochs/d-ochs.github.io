Daniel Ochs

AIML Lab @ Technische Universität Darmstadt

Hey, my name is Daniel. I am a PhD student at the Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning Lab of TU Darmstadt. My research is centered on bringing symbolic and neural systems closer to solve challenging problems like understanding and reasoning about visual scenes. Currently, I am working on the advancement of deep probabilistic programming languages. I am supervised by Prof. Kristian Kersting and Dr. Devendra Singh Dhami.
